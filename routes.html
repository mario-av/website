Routes

Our Curated Routes

Four handpicked journeys through Denmark's most iconic destinations. Each route offers a unique perspective on Danish culture, history, and natural beauty.

The Copenhagen Classic

On Foot • Capital Experience

Discover the heart of Denmark in its vibrant capital—design, history, and gastronomy.

Day 1 — Nyhavn & Strøget: Start at the colorful harbor of Nyhavn, then explore Europe's longest pedestrian street with its shops and cafés.

Day 2 — Tivoli & Design: Spend the morning at Tivoli Gardens, then visit the Design Museum Denmark for Scandinavian aesthetics.

Day 3 — Castles & Cuisine: Tour Amalienborg Palace, try smørrebrød at Torvehallerne market, and end with a canal boat tour.

The Aarhus Art Trail

By Train • Culture & Design

Denmark's second city is a hotspot for modern art, architecture, and New Nordic cuisine.

Day 1 — ARoS & Rainbow: Start at ARoS Art Museum and walk through Olafur Eliasson's "Your Rainbow Panorama" for 360° city views.

Day 2 — Den Gamle By: Step into The Old Town, an open-air museum with 75 historic buildings from across Denmark.

Day 3 — Moesgaard & Coast: Visit Moesgaard Museum for Viking artifacts, then hike the rooftop trail through forest to the bay.

The White Cliffs of Møn

By Foot • Nature & Hiking

Dramatic chalk cliffs, ancient forests, and Denmark's best stargazing await on this island escape.

Day 1 — Møns Klint: Hike the stunning 128-meter chalk cliffs rising from the Baltic Sea. Visit GeoCenter to explore 70 million years of history.

Day 2 — Liselund & Forests: Explore the romantic 18th-century Liselund Park, then hike through the ancient beech forests of Klinteskoven.

Night 2 — Dark Sky Park: Return at night to Denmark's first certified Dark Sky Park for spectacular Milky Way views and meteor showers.

The LEGO & Lions Loop

By Car • Family Adventure

The ultimate family trip through central Jutland—theme parks, wildlife, and imagination.

Day 1 — LEGO House: Spend the day at "The Home of the Brick" in Billund. Lunch at Mini Chef where robots serve your meal!

Day 2 — LEGOLAND: Theme park adventures await. Don't miss Miniland, where famous landmarks are recreated in millions of bricks.

Day 3 — Givskud Zoo: Drive your own car on safari among lions and giraffes. End at Ribe VikingeCenter for archery and bread baking.

Beyond Denmark

The Danish Realm extends far into the North Atlantic. Discover the remote wonders of Greenland and the Faroe Islands.

Greenland Expedition

By Air • Arctic Adventure • 5 Days

From Copenhagen to the world's largest island—icebergs, Northern Lights, and Inuit culture.

Day 1 — Copenhagen to Kangerlussuaq: Fly from Copenhagen. Land at Greenland's main airport and explore the arctic tundra around the ice cap.

Day 2 — Ilulissat Icefjord: Fly to Ilulissat. Boat tour through UNESCO's Icefjord watching house-sized icebergs calve from the glacier.

Day 3 — Dog Sledding: Traditional Greenlandic dog sled ride across the frozen landscape. Visit a local Inuit community.

Day 4 — Northern Lights: Midnight excursion to hunt the Aurora Borealis. Daytime hike to the edge of the ice sheet.

Day 5 — Return: Final morning in the arctic before flying back to Copenhagen via Kangerlussuaq.

Faroe Islands Discovery

By Air • Island Escape • 4 Days

18 volcanic islands with dramatic cliffs, grass-roofed villages, and untouched Nordic nature.

Day 1 — Copenhagen to Vágar: 2-hour flight to Vágar Airport. Drive to Sørvágsvatn, the "floating lake" optical illusion, then to Tórshavn.

Day 2 — Múlafossur Waterfall: Hike to the iconic waterfall plunging into the ocean at Gásadalur. Afternoon in colorful Tórshavn old town.

Day 3 — Mykines & Puffins: Ferry to Mykines island for puffin watching on the dramatic sea cliffs. One of Europe's best birdwatching spots.

Day 4 — Return: Morning at Kirkjubøur's medieval ruins and grass-roofed houses before flying back to Copenhagen.
