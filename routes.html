Beyond Denmark

The Danish Realm extends far into the North Atlantic. Explore the remote wonders of Greenland and the Faroe Islands.

Greenland

The world's largest island, where icebergs the size of buildings drift past colorful villages. Experience dog sledding, witness the Northern Lights, and explore Inuit culture.

  • Ilulissat Icefjord (UNESCO)
  • Traditional dog sledding
  • Northern Lights viewing

Faroe Islands
