White Cliffs of Møn

3 Days
Hiking
Dark Sky Park
1

Day 1: Geology & Giants

Begin at GeoCenter Møns Klint to understand the 70-million-year history of the chalk cliffs. Then, take the long staircase down to the beach to hunt for fossils and look up at the towering white walls rising 128 meters above the sea.

2

Day 2: Romantic Gardens

Explore Liselund Park, a romantic 18th-century garden with thatched cottages, lakes, and winding paths. It feels like a fairy tale. In the afternoon, hike through the ancient beech forests of Klinteskoven.

3

Day 3: Stargazing

Stay up late to experience one of the best spots in the world for stargazing. Møn is a certified Dark Sky Park, meaning light pollution is minimal. On clear nights, the Milky Way is breathtakingly visible.

Helpful Info

  • Access: Best by car (1.5h from CPH)
  • Trails: All levels (many stairs)
  • Cliffs: Free access
GeoCenter Info

Accommodation

Stay at the specialized 'Dark Sky' B&Bs or camping sites (Camp Møns Klint) for the full experience.
