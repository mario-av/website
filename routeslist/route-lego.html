LEGO & Lions Loop

3 Days
Family Friendly
Road Trip
1

Day 1: Home of the Brick

Arrive in Billund and spend the day at LEGO House. It's an interactive experience center where you can build anything you imagine. Don't miss the massive Tree of Creativity and have lunch at Mini Chef, where robots serve your LEGO-brick meal.

2

Day 2: LEGOLAND Adventure

A full day at the original LEGOLAND park. Explore Miniland where world landmarks are built from 20 million bricks, ride the Dragon coaster, and get your first driving license at the Toyota Traffic School.

3

Day 3: Safari & Vikings

Drive your own car through Givskud Zoo to see lions and giraffes up close. Afterwards, head to the Ribe VikingeCenter to bake bread, shoot arrows, and learn about the real Vikings in Denmark's oldest town.

Visitor Info

  • Airport: Billund (BLL)
  • Season: LEGOLAND (Apr-Oct)
  • LEGO House: Open All Year
Experience Billund

Stay & Play

For the ultimate experience, stay at the LEGOLAND Castle Hotel or Hotel LEGOLAND - just steps from the park.
