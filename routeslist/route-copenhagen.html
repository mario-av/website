City Break

The Copenhagen Classic

3 Days
Guided / Self-Guided
Copenhagen & Surroundings
1

Day 1: The Heart of the City

Arrive in Copenhagen and settle into your boutique hotel near Kongens Nytorv. Start your journey with a canal tour from Nyhavn, seeing the Opera House and the Little Mermaid from the water. In the afternoon, walk down Strøget and climb the Round Tower for panoramic views.

2

Day 2: Royal Palaces & Design

Visit Amalienborg Palace at noon for the Changing of the Guard. Then, immerse yourself in Danish aesthetics at the Design Museum. Spend your evening at the magical Tivoli Gardens, one of the world's oldest amusement parks.

3

Day 3: Nordic Flavors & Hygge

Start with a food tour at Torvehallerne market, tasting Smørrebrød and Danish pastries. Rent a bike like a local and cycle to the hipster district of Nørrebro or visit the alternative freetown of Christiania before your departure.

Practical Info

  • Currency: Danish Krone (DKK)
  • Language: Danish (English widely spoken)
  • Plugs: Type K (230V)
  • Transport: Metro, Bus, & Bike
