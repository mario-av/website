The Aarhus Art Trail

3 Days
Accessible by Train
Museums & History
1

Day 1: Rainbow City

Arrive in Aarhus, Denmark's second-largest city. Head straight to ARoS Art Museum, one of the largest in Northern Europe. Walk inside "Your Rainbow Panorama" by Olafur Eliasson for a spectacular 360-degree color-filtered view of the city.

2

Day 2: Time Travel

Step back in time at "Den Gamle By" (The Old Town). This open-air museum features 75 historic buildings relocated from all over Denmark. Experience life as it was in the 1800s, 1920s, and 1970s, complete with actors in period costume.

3

Day 3: Prehistory & Nature

Visit the architectural marvel of Moesgaard Museum. See the Grauballe Man, the world's best-preserved bog body. Afterwards, walk on the museum's sloping grass roof and hike through the surrounding forests down to Moesgaard Beach.

Practical Info

  • From CPH: 3 hours by train
  • Airport: Aarhus (AAR)
  • Walkability: High
